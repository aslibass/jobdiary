# JobDiary Quick Start Guide

**Get up and running in 2 minutes!**

## Step 1: Open the App

Navigate to your JobDiary URL (provided by your administrator).

## Step 2: Allow Microphone Access

When you first click the microphone button, your browser will ask for permission. Click **"Allow"**.

## Step 3: Record Your First Entry

1. **Click the blue microphone button** (large circle)
2. **Wait for "Recording..."** to appear
3. **Speak your entry**, for example:
   > "Finished painting the kitchen today. Used 3 gallons of Behr eggshell. Need to touch up the trim tomorrow."
4. **Click the red square** to stop recording
5. **Review the transcript** (you can edit if needed)
6. **Click "Save Entry"**

That's it! Your entry is saved.

## Tips for Best Results

- **Speak clearly** at a normal pace
- **Be specific**: Mention colors, quantities, and areas
- **Use common terms**: "cut in", "rolled", "sprayed", "primed"
- **Check the transcript** before saving
