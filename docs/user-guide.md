## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Open the App**: Navigate to your JobDiary URL (provided by your administrator)

2. **Dark Mode Toggle**: Click the sun/moon icon in the top right to switch between light and dark mode
   - **Dark mode** is recommended for low-light job sites
   - **Light mode** works well in bright sunlight

3. **Microphone Permission**: When you first record, your browser will ask for microphone permission
   - Click **"Allow"** to enable voice recording
   - This permission is required for the app to work

### Understanding the Interface

The JobDiary interface has three main sections:

1. **Voice Recorder** (top): Record your daily entries
2. **Jobs List** (left): View and select your active jobs
3. **Entries List** (right): See all entries for the selected job

## Recording Voice Entries

### Basic Recording

1. **Start Recording**:
   - Click the large microphone button (blue circle)
   - Wait for "Connecting..." message to disappear
   - When you see "Recording... Speak now" in red, start speaking

2. **While Recording**:
   - Speak clearly and naturally
   - The app will transcribe your speech in real-time
   - You'll see:
     - **Timer**: Shows how long you've been recording (MM:SS)
     - **Audio Level**: Green bar shows microphone input level
     - **Transcript**: Your words appear as you speak

3. **Stop Recording**:
   - Click the red square button to stop
   - The transcript will remain in the text box

4. **Review & Edit**:
   - Check the transcript for accuracy
   - You can type to edit or add details
   - The word count is shown below the text box

5. **Save Entry**:
   - Click **"Save Entry"** button
   - Your entry will be saved to the currently selected job
   - If no job is selected, a new job will be created automatically

### What to Say

JobDiary automatically extracts important information from your voice entries. Here are examples:

#### Example 1: End of Day Summary
> "Finished painting the kitchen walls today. Used 3 gallons of Behr eggshell in SW 7008. Cut in all the edges and rolled the walls. Need to touch up the trim tomorrow."

**What gets extracted:**
- **Areas**: kitchen walls
- **Colors**: Behr eggshell, SW 7008
- **Techniques**: cut in, rolled
- **Materials**: 3 gallons
- **Next Actions**: touch up trim tomorrow

#### Example 2: Issue Logging
> "Found bleed through in the bedroom corner. Need to prime that area before painting. Used 2 gallons of primer today."

**What gets extracted:**
- **Issues**: bleed through in bedroom corner
- **Areas**: bedroom
- **Materials**: 2 gallons of primer
- **Next Actions**: prime before painting

#### Example 3: Quick Update
> "Completed the exterior trim. Sprayed with Sherwin Williams Duration, semi-gloss finish. All done with this job."

**What gets extracted:**
- **Areas**: exterior trim
- **Techniques**: sprayed
- **Colors**: Sherwin Williams Duration, semi-gloss
- **Status**: job complete

### Auto-Save Feature

- Your transcript is **automatically saved** as a draft while you work
- If you close the app or your phone dies, your draft will be restored when you return
- Drafts are kept for 24 hours
- Once you save an entry, the draft is cleared

### Viewing Jobs

- **Job Name**: The name or address of the job
- **Status Badge**: 
  - üü¢ Green = Complete
  - üîµ Blue = In Progress
  - ‚ö™ Gray = Not Started

### Selecting a Job

- Click on any job in the list to view its entries
- The selected job is highlighted in blue
- Entries for that job appear on the right

### Creating a New Job

Jobs are created automatically when you:
1. Record a voice entry without a job selected
2. Mention a job name or address in your entry

**Example**: Say "New job at 123 Main Street" and the app will create a job called "123 Main Street"

### Job Status

Job status is automatically updated based on your entries:
- **In Progress**: Has recent entries
- **Complete**: You mention completion in an entry

## Viewing Entries

### Entry Display

Each entry shows:

1. **Timestamp**: When the entry was created
2. **Transcript**: Your full voice entry text
3. **Extracted Data** (if available):
   - **Areas**: Rooms or areas painted
   - **Colors**: Paint colors and brands
   - **Techniques**: Methods used (cut in, rolled, sprayed, etc.)
   - **Materials**: Quantities and items used
   - **Completed**: Tasks finished
   - **Next**: Upcoming tasks
   - **Issues**: Problems or concerns (shown in red)

### Entry Organization

- Entries are shown in **reverse chronological order** (newest first)
- Each entry is in its own card
- Click **"Refresh"** to reload entries if needed

### Searching Entries

Currently, entries are organized by job. To find a specific entry:
1. Select the job from the Jobs List
2. Scroll through entries on the right
3. Use your browser's search (Ctrl+F or Cmd+F) to find keywords

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Painters

#### Daily Workflow

1. **Start of Day**: Record what you plan to do
   > "Starting on the living room today. Will cut in and roll the walls with Behr eggshell."

2. **During Work**: Log issues as they come up
   > "Found peeling paint in the bathroom. Need to scrape and prime before painting."

3. **End of Day**: Summarize what was completed
   > "Finished kitchen and dining room. Used 4 gallons total. Need to touch up trim tomorrow."

#### Material Tracking

Always mention quantities and brands:
- ‚úÖ "Used 3 gallons of Sherwin Williams Duration"
- ‚úÖ "Need 2 more brushes and a roller"
- ‚ùå "Used some paint" (too vague)

#### Issue Documentation

Be specific about problems:
- ‚úÖ "Bleed through in bedroom corner, needs primer"
- ‚úÖ "Peeling paint on exterior trim, needs scraping"
- ‚ùå "There's a problem" (not helpful)

### Mobile Use

#### On Job Sites

- **Use Dark Mode**: Better visibility in low-light areas
- **Large Buttons**: Designed to work with gloved hands
- **Voice-First**: Hands-free operation when your hands are dirty
- **Auto-Save**: Don't worry about losing your work

#### Connectivity

- **Draft Saving**: Works offline - drafts are saved locally
- **Auto-Restore**: Drafts are restored when you reconnect
- **No Data Loss**: Your entries are saved even if connection drops

### Best Practices

1. **Be Specific**: Mention exact colors, quantities, and areas
2. **Log Issues Immediately**: Don't wait - record problems as you find them
3. **End-of-Day Summaries**: Great for tracking progress
4. **Use Consistent Terms**: Helps with automatic extraction
5. **Review Transcripts**: Check accuracy before saving

## Troubleshooting

### Voice Recording Issues

#### "Failed to get ephemeral token"
- **Cause**: Connection issue or server problem
- **Solution**: 
  - Check your internet connection
  - Wait a few seconds and try again
  - Refresh the page if problem persists

#### "Microphone not working"
- **Cause**: Browser permission denied or microphone not connected
- **Solution**:
  1. Check browser settings - allow microphone access
  2. Make sure microphone is connected (for external mics)
  3. Try refreshing the page
  4. Check if other apps can use your microphone

#### "No audio level showing"
- **Cause**: Microphone not picking up sound
- **Solution**:
  - Check microphone volume settings
  - Move closer to microphone
  - Check if microphone is muted
  - Try speaking louder

#### "Transcript not appearing"
- **Cause**: Connection issue or speech not detected
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Speak more clearly and louder
  - Check audio level indicator - should show green when speaking
  - Wait a few seconds - transcription may be delayed

### Saving Issues

#### "Failed to save entry"
- **Cause**: Connection issue or server error
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Your draft is saved locally - try again later
  - Check if you have a job selected
  - Refresh the page and try again

#### "Entry not showing up"
- **Cause**: Need to refresh or wrong job selected
- **Solution**:
  - Click "Refresh" button on the Entries list
  - Check that the correct job is selected
  - Wait a few seconds - entries may take a moment to appear

### Display Issues

#### "Can't see text clearly"
- **Solution**:
  - Toggle dark/light mode (sun/moon icon)
  - Adjust your device brightness
  - Check if your browser zoom is too high/low

#### "Layout looks wrong"
- **Solution**:
  - Refresh the page
  - Check browser zoom level (should be 100%)
  - Try a different browser if problem persists

### General Issues

#### "App is slow"
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection speed
  - Close other browser tabs
  - Clear browser cache
  - Try refreshing the page

#### "Lost my draft"
- **Solution**:
  - Drafts are saved for 24 hours
  - Check if you're on the same device/browser
  - Drafts are stored locally - clearing browser data will delete them

## Advanced Features

### Data Extraction

JobDiary automatically extracts structured data from your voice entries:

- **Areas**: Kitchen, bedroom, exterior, trim, etc.
- **Colors**: Paint colors, brands, finish types
- **Techniques**: Cut in, rolled, sprayed, primed, sanded, taped
- **Materials**: Quantities and items (gallons, brushes, rollers)
- **Issues**: Problems that need attention
- **Tasks**: Completed work and next actions

This extracted data makes it easy to:
- Track material usage
- Identify issues quickly
- See what's been completed
- Plan next steps

### Dark Mode

**When to use Dark Mode:**
- Working in low-light areas
- Reducing eye strain
- Saving battery on OLED screens
- Personal preference

**When to use Light Mode:**
- Working in bright sunlight
- Better contrast in daylight
- Personal preference

Toggle anytime using the sun/moon icon in the top right.
